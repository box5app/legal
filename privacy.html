Introduction

Box 5 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Box 5 (the "App"). By using Box 5, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information: Name and basic profile information you provide during onboarding, learning preferences and daily goals, and user-generated flashcard content (text and images).

User-Generated Content: We store the content you create within the App, including flashcard decks and cards (text and images), study progress and statistics, and learning history.

Automatically Collected Information: We use PostHog to automatically collect anonymized usage data, including device type and operating system version, app usage statistics, feature interactions, and performance data to help us improve the App.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide and maintain the App, store your flashcards and study progress locally on your device, track your learning progress using the Leitner spaced repetition system, improve and optimize the App, and respond to your support requests.

Data Storage

Local Storage: All your flashcard data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. This includes your flashcards, decks, study progress, statistics, and learning history. Your personal study data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

No Cloud Storage: Box 5 does not require an account and does not store your flashcard data on remote servers. All your study-related data remains on your device, giving you complete privacy and control.

Analytics Data: Anonymized usage statistics are processed by PostHog to help us understand how the App is used and to improve its performance.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Expo for app development platform (see Expo Privacy Policy at expo.dev/privacy)
  • PostHog for product analytics and application monitoring (see PostHog Privacy Policy at posthog.com/privacy)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your flashcard data is stored locally on your device and is not shared. We share anonymized usage analytics with PostHog, our analytics provider, to help us maintain and improve the App.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the App settings. Upon account deletion, your authentication data will be removed from our servers. Local data on your device will remain until you uninstall the App.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your flashcard data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Children's Privacy

Box 5 is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication through OAuth 2.0
  • Regular security updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
