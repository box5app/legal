Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data backed up?

Box 5 stores all your data locally on your device for maximum privacy. We highly recommend generating regular backups using the "Export Data" feature in the app settings to ensure your flashcards are safe.

How does the Leitner System work?

Cards start in Box 1. Correst answers move a card to the next box (increasing the review interval). Incorrect answers move it back to Box 1. This ensures you spend more time on cards you struggle with.

Is Box 5 free?

Yes, Box 5 is currently free to use.
